Privacy Policy for MyWebClass.org

mywebclass.org was created and meant for the use of the IS218 class at NJIT. This course is being taught by Professor Keith Williams. This policy page is used to inform visitors of our rules and regulations regarding our site. Information collected will not be shared amongst anyone else but stored in the privacy section

The information gathered will only be associated with your binary code and will not be shared with anyone else as stated in the policy

Information we collected

When you use our website, we may request you to provide certain personal information that can be used to contact or recognize you. This personal information may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other related information.

Apart from this, we may also gather information that cannot be used to identify you personally, regarding your visit to the website, such as the duration of your stay on the website, the pages you visited, and the websites you accessed before and after our website. This information is collected through Google Analytics and cookies.

Google Analytics

To gain insights into how visitors use our website, we rely on Google Analytics. By collecting information about user behavior, we can generate reports that guide us in improving our site's performance. The data obtained through Google Analytics is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. The information includes device and browser type, location, viewed pages, and visit duration. By studying this information, we can enhance your browsing experience on our website.

We use cookies to store small data files on your device. These files are made up of anonymous unique identifiers and can hold additional information. They are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. You have the option to block all cookies or receive alerts whenever a cookie is sent. Nonetheless, restricting cookies may limit your access to certain parts of our website. By allowing cookies, we can enhance your browsing experience and personalize your interaction with our website.

Security

We take the security of your Personal Information seriously and use appropriate measures to protect it. However, we cannot fully guarantee the safety of security as no method of transmitting or storing data electronically is completely secure. However, we strive to make sure your information is safe

Links to third party sites

In this site, you will most likely see different url links in which will take you to different pages. These pages will give you a more thorough breakdown of what there is to come

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Our site reserves the right to make changes to our Privacy Policy without prior notice. Any updates will be reflected by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We advise you to check this page regularly to stay up-to-date on any changes. All revisions to this Privacy Policy will become effective immediately upon their posting on this page. We believe that it is important to keep our Privacy Policy current to reflect any changes in the way we collect, use, and protect your Personal Information.
